Our Mission

At Yomping of Yeovil, our mission is to encourage people of all ages and abilities to explore Somerset’s countryside safely and confidently. We aim to promote physical wellbeing, respect for the natural environment, and a deeper connection to local landscapes through friendly, professionally guided walking experiences.

Our History

Yomping of Yeovil began as a small community walking group formed by local outdoor enthusiasts with a shared love for Somerset’s hills and countryside. What initially started as informal weekend walks grew steadily as more people expressed interest in exploring the area with experienced leaders.

Over time, the group developed into a structured outdoor activity company, offering organised guided walks across the Mendip Hills, Quantock Hills, Exmoor National Park, and Ham Hill Country Park. Today, Yomping of Yeovil remains focused on inclusivity, safety, and enjoyment of the outdoors.
