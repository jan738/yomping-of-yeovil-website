Emergency Information

  • Emergency Services: 999 or 112
  • NHS 111: 111 (non‑emergency medical advice)

In the event of an emergency during any walk, guides will contact the appropriate services and ensure group members are kept safe.

Safety Procedures

  • All walks are led by experienced guides.
  • Participants must follow guide instructions at all times.
  • Regular rest stops are planned into each walk.
  • Weather conditions are checked before every activity.
  • Any medical conditions should be declared before the walk.

What to Wear & Bring

  • Sturdy walking boots or shoes with good grip
  • Weather‑appropriate clothing and waterproof jacket
  • Warm layers during colder months
  • Sunscreen and hat in warm weather
  • Sufficient water and snacks

Cancellation Policy

We understand that plans can change. Our cancellation policy is designed to be clear and fair.

  • Cancellations made more than 48 hours before the walk will receive a full refund.
  • Cancellations made within 48 hours of the walk are non‑refundable.
  • If a walk is cancelled due to severe weather, an alternative date will be offered.
